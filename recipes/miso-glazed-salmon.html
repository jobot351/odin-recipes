Air Fryer Miso-Glazed Salmon

Simple And Delicious

This miso glaze in this simple air fryer salmon recipe creates the perfect balance of salty, sweet, and heat. With only 5 ingredients, it comes together quickly enough for an everyday meal but is so good, you’ll want to serve it for company, too. If you're looking for bold flavors, look no further.

Ingredients

  • 2 (6 ounce) salmon filets
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon white miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Place salmon fillets in a flat dish.
  2. Whisk brown sugar, miso paste, soy sauce, and chili flakes in a bowl. Pour mixture over salmon fillets. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray basket with cooking spray.
  4. Using tongs, place salmon filets skin side down in the basket of the air fryer, making sure to reserve the excess marinade. Cook 4 minutes. Baste filets with reserved marinade and cook 4 minutes more.
